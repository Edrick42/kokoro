# Kokoro — Monetization Strategy

> Multiple revenue streams from one project. The game, the book, the content, and the community.

---

## 1. The Game (Primary Product)

### Model: Freemium Mobile App

**Free tier:**
- 1 creature (Moluun, the most accessible)
- Full lifecycle: egg → elder
- Basic food types
- Core interaction (feed, play, sleep, pet)

**Premium unlock ($4.99 one-time):**
- All 4 species
- Expanded food/nutrition system
- Cosmetic accessories and environments
- Web profile sync
- No ads, ever

**Why one-time, not subscription:** Virtual pet players build emotional bonds. A subscription creates anxiety ("if I stop paying, my pet dies?"). One-time purchase respects the relationship.

### Platforms & Revenue Split
| Platform | Revenue Share | Notes |
|----------|--------------|-------|
| iOS (App Store) | 70% to you | $99/year developer account |
| Android (Google Play) | 85% to you (first $1M) | $25 one-time registration |
| Desktop (itch.io) | 90-100% to you | No approval process |
| Desktop (Steam) | 70% to you | $100 per game listing |

### Pricing Strategy
- Launch at $4.99 (impulse buy, no friction)
- Bundle with ebook for $14.99 ("Game + Learn Rust" pack)
- Seasonal cosmetic packs at $1.99 (holiday accessories, biome themes)

## 2. The Ebook/Course (Education Product)

### "Building Kokoro: Learn Rust by Creating Life"

**Format options:**

| Platform | Price Range | Pros | Cons |
|----------|-------------|------|------|
| **Gumroad** | $19-39 | Easy setup, you keep 90%+, pay-what-you-want option | Less discoverability |
| **Leanpub** | $19-39 | Built for tech books, incremental publishing, 80% royalty | Niche audience |
| **Amazon Kindle** (KDP) | $9.99-14.99 | Massive audience, Kindle Unlimited | 35-70% royalty, formatting pain |
| **Udemy** (video course) | $19.99-49.99 | Huge audience, frequent sales | 37% on organic, they own the pricing |
| **Your own site** | $29-49 | 100% revenue, full control | Need marketing |

### Recommended Approach
1. **Start on Gumroad** — publish as you write, early access pricing ($9.99)
2. **Raise price** as chapters are added ($19.99 at 50%, $29.99 at 100%)
3. **Add to Amazon KDP** when complete (wider reach)
4. **Create video companion** on YouTube (free) → drives book sales
5. **Bundle** game + book on your own site

### Unique Selling Points
- Not just "learn Rust" — build something real and emotional
- Every chapter produces working code
- The creature you build in the book is the same one in the actual game
- Exercises that accumulate into a complete game
- Biological simulation teaches data modeling, ECS, neural networks

### Target Audience
- Developers learning Rust (huge growing market)
- Game dev enthusiasts wanting a unique project
- CS students looking for a portfolio project
- People who loved Tamagotchi and want to understand how it works

## 3. YouTube / Content (Marketing + Revenue)

### Channel Strategy: Dev Log + Tutorial Hybrid

**Content pillars:**

| Series | Format | Frequency | Purpose |
|--------|--------|-----------|---------|
| **"Building Kokoro"** dev logs | 10-15 min | Weekly/biweekly | Show progress, build audience, human connection |
| **"Rust Through Kokoro"** tutorials | 15-25 min | Weekly | Teach concepts from the book, drive sales |
| **"Creature AI Deep Dives"** | 20-30 min | Monthly | Neural networks, genetics, biology — the juicy stuff |
| **Shorts / Reels** | 30-60 sec | 3x/week | Creature behavior clips, "watch my pet learn" moments |

### Revenue from YouTube
| Source | When | Revenue |
|--------|------|---------|
| YouTube Partner (ads) | After 1K subs + 4K hours | $3-8 per 1K views |
| Channel memberships | After 1K subs | $2-5/month per member |
| Super Thanks | Immediately | Tips on videos |
| Affiliate links | Immediately | Book/course links in description |

### Content That Goes Viral
- "My AI pet learned to refuse food it doesn't like"
- "I left my virtual creature alone for 7 days. Here's what happened."
- "Building a neural network for a pet game in Rust"
- "This creature has a heartbeat. I coded it from scratch."
- "The biology behind my virtual pet game"

## 4. Crowdfunding (Kickstarter / Indiegogo)

### When to Launch
**After MVP is playable** — people need to see the creature alive, breathing, reacting. A video of the egg hatching, the creature learning your name, the kokoro-sac glowing... that's the pitch.

### Campaign Structure

**Goal: $5,000-15,000** (realistic for indie game + book)

| Tier | Price | Reward |
|------|-------|--------|
| Supporter | $5 | Digital wallpaper + name in credits |
| Caretaker | $15 | Full game (all species) + ebook early access |
| Breeder | $25 | Game + ebook + exclusive species (5th species Kickstarter-only) |
| Researcher | $50 | All above + behind-the-scenes dev docs + Discord role |
| Creator | $100 | All above + name a food item or accessory in-game |
| Patron | $250 | All above + design input on a species trait + signed postcard |

### Stretch Goals
- $10K: Sound design and ambient music
- $15K: Mobile port (iOS + Android)
- $20K: Online breeding (P2P)
- $30K: Spanish/Portuguese/Japanese localization

## Key Insight

Kokoro is not just a game. It's an **ecosystem**:

```
Game (emotional bond) → YouTube (discovery) → Book (education) → Community (retention)
     ↑                                                                    |
     └────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

Each product feeds the others. Someone discovers Kokoro through a YouTube short of a creature learning to refuse food. They download the game. They get curious about how it works. They buy the book. They join the community. They tell their friends.

The creature is the hook. The biology is the depth. The Rust education is the lasting value.
